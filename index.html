What is Haikyu

Haikyu is a Japanese volleyball manga series written and illustrated Haruichi Faledate. This manga/anime is about a short boy named Shoyo Hinata so he can become the best volleyball player but he is short so its gonna be challenging for him. Hinata shoyo wanted to be like a volleyball championship player, a short player nicknamed "the small giant" In third and final year of junior high, some of Hinata's friends agree to join the club so he can compete in a tournament.
